Overview

  • Founder: Arjeigh Ramos
  • Established: December 2021
  • Location: Roxas, Calapan City, Oriental Mindoro

Mission

The Krusada Project is dedicated to merging art with the Word of God, aiming to create meaningful connections between faith and creativity. Arjeigh’s vision is to produce artwork that resonates deeply with individuals, fostering a sense of spiritual reflection and inspiration.

Product Range

  • Tsuno Bags: Stylish and functional bags that are not only practical but also carry messages of faith and purpose.
  • Keychains: Thoughtfully designed keychains that serve as daily reminders of one's connection to God.
  • Shirts: Fashionable apparel featuring designs that communicate powerful biblical messages and themes.
  • Stickers: Vibrant stickers that can be used to personalize belongings while spreading messages of hope and faith.
  • Other Artwork: Unique pieces that incorporate various artistic styles and mediums, all centered around themes of spirituality and devotion.

Artistic Vision

Each creation is inspired by Arjeigh's personal journey with the Lord, making the art not just a product but a reflection of his faith. The designs often draw from scripture, incorporating verses and spiritual motifs that speak to the heart and soul. This approach allows customers to connect with the artwork on a personal level, seeing it as both an aesthetic choice and a source of inspiration.

Community Engagement

The Krusada Project emphasizes building a community of believers who appreciate the intersection of art and faith. Arjeigh aims to engage with customers through workshops, events, and social media, creating spaces for dialogue and connection around shared values and beliefs.

Purpose

The Krusada Project seeks to inspire and encourage individuals through art that conveys profound messages of faith. By sharing his work, Arjeigh hopes to spark conversations about spirituality, cultivate a deeper understanding of one’s relationship with God, and encourage others on their personal journeys of faith. Each product serves as a reminder that art can be a powerful vehicle for expressing and celebrating one’s beliefs.
